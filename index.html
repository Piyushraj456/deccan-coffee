Our Story

Bringing the Soul of South to Noida

At Deccan Coffee Club, we believe coffee is more than a beverage — it's a ritual, a memory, a warm embrace. Rooted in the coffee culture of Bangalore and the Deccan region, we bring authentic filter coffee to the heart of Noida.

Every cup is freshly brewed from carefully sourced beans, served the traditional way — tumbler, davara, and all. Our food follows the same philosophy: honest, flavorful, and deeply South Indian.
